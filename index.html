Services

The services Prunty Tree and Crane LLC include: pruning, cabling, removal, and even general crane work. Pruning should be done regularly to reduce the chance of limb or tree failure. If done early and often it can improve the overall health and structure of the tree. Cabling should be done if a limb has an above average risk for failure, this should be up to the arborist. When pruning and/or cabling efforts have been exhausted then removal may be the only option. We can do that too!
